Modeling & Detailing

Essential Course

Tekla Steel Detailing

Our Tekla Steel Detailing course covers all the essential aspects of modelling steel structures and creating drawings, reports and delivering packages with a proper work flow. During the training, candidates will learn how to model, practice 25 different types of the most commonly used connections, as well as how to handle revisions, create reports, shop and erection drawings and deliver packages. Though it is a steel detailing training, basic concrete elements cannot be disregarded. So, you will also learn how to create footing, slabs, panels, reinforcements, and precast components as a part of this steel detailing course.
